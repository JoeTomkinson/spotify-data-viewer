# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in contributing.

## Contributor License Agreement Required

All contributions to this repository are subject to our Contributor License Agreement (CLA). By submitting a Pull Request, you agree to grant the project maintainer the right to relicense your contribution under commercial terms. You retain copyright ownership of your work, but you provide us the necessary rights to distribute it legally.

## Pull Request Policy

- Every Pull Request must pass the automated CLA check before it can be merged.
- If the CLA check reports that you have not yet signed, follow the instructions posted on the Pull Request and submit the required confirmation comment.
- Contributions that are submitted without agreeing to the CLA cannot be accepted.

## Contribution Expectations

- Keep changes focused and clearly described.
- Confirm that your contribution is your original work or that you have the legal right to submit it.
- Do not submit code, assets, or text that you are not authorized to license under the repository terms.
